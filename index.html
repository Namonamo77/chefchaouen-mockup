Chefchaouen Hills

Welcome to the blue city

Nestled in the Rif mountains of Morocco, Chefchaouen, also known as the "Blue Pearl," is a breathtaking town famed for its vibrant, blue-washed buildings. This unique color scheme adorns the houses in the winding medina, creating a picturesque labyrinth of narrow streets. Beyond the visual appeal, Chefchaouen offers a rich cultural experience, with its historic Kasbah, a 15th century fortress, and the Grand Mosque with its distinctive octagonal minaret. Soak up the atmosphere in the lively Outa el Hammam square, a central hub bustling with cafes and vendors.
